Welcome to Janaki Montessori School

About Us

Our Philosophy

Janaki Montessori School is a child-centered, progressive educational institution located in Itahari-17, Pakali, Sunsari. Established with the aim of nurturing young minds in a loving and inspiring environment, the school follows Montessori principles that promote independence, creativity, and holistic development.

Our Approach

We provide a safe, clean, and joyful learning space for children to explore, discover, and grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially. Our dedicated team of trained educators ensures that each child receives personalized attention and encouragement in their learning journey.

Message from the Principal

Dear Parents and Guardians,
Welcome to Janaki Montessori School!
We believe every child is special and should learn in a safe and caring environment. Using the Montessori method, we help children learn by doing, explore their interests, and grow in every way. Our kind and experienced teachers are here to guide and support your child.
Thank you for trusting us with your child’s learning.
Sincerely,

Principal Mamata Sinjali

Our Commitment

To become a leading early childhood educational institution in Nepal by fostering curiosity, independence, and a lifelong love for learning in every child.
